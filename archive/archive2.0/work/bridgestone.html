Bridgestone Tire

Bridgestone Tire launched a brand new design and infrastructure of their website earlier this year. They wanted something modern, sleek, and could last for years to come, and we mirrored those ideals with our coding practices. Notably, the site features an advanced tire filtering system in the tire catalog and global search, is fully responsive from mobile, tablet, to desktop, and built in Adobe's latest version (6.0) of AEM. My role included, maintenance, measurement, full component creation and integration into the cms, and specific owning of the "miscellaneous" pages.
