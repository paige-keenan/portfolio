American Red Cross

I redesigned the Red Cross's website with the objective to encourage viewers of this site to donate money. To acheive this, I used emotional imagery and chose thought provoking colors. The site was divided into four main sections: a donations section, a needy nearby section, a how to help section, and a request assistance section. Each section directed and alerted the users of ways to help and gave the opporutunity for users to donate funds.
