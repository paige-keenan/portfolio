Atlanta Humane Society

This site was created to promote pet adoptions in Atlanta and is divided into four main sections to do so. These sections include: a section to show which animals are availble for adoption, a satistics sections to provide information about pet adoptions, a section to show what comes with an animal adoption, and a contact section. This site is fully responsive, features pure CSS animations, and animations triggered upon scrolling to their perspective sections.
