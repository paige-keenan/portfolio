Kotori

When Unity first started to become popular, I teamed up with some friends, and we created a video game together. Our game was called Kotori and featured a half-human / half-bird protagonist who was fighting to bring color back into the world. We took more of a puzzle approach to completing the game (as apposed to an action packed, fight the villian game) where the character may lose health a few times before he knows how to solve a certain obstacle. As the character draws closer to completing the game, gradually more colors begin to enter into the world.
